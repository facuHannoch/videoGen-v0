The middle vowel /ɛ/ is the key part—keep your lips relaxed, lower your jaw slightly, and let the tongue stay in the front-middle area of the mouth. This helps you say bed clearly and avoid mixing it up with bad or bid. Great for English pronunciation practice, IPA learning, and accent training.
