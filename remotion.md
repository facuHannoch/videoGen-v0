### Sintethize them





  - Mixkit (free SFX + music, simple licensing): https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
    and license hub: https://mixkit.co/license/
  - Pixabay (free SFX/music under Pixabay content license):
    https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/ and license summary:
    https://pixabay.com/service/license-summary/
  - Freesound (huge library, but each file can be CC0, CC BY, or CC BY-NC):
    https://freesound.org/help/faq/
  - ZapSplat (large SFX library; free tier generally requires attribution):
    https://www.zapsplat.com/ and credit rules:
    https://www.zapsplat.com/how-to-credit-us/
  - Uppbeat (free + paid plans for music/SFX): https://uppbeat.io/pricing


For “download a bundle and use it,” these are the best options:

  - Sonniss GDC bundles (huge yearly packs, royalty-free):
    https://sonniss.com/gameaudiogdc/
    Example bundle page: https://gdc.sonniss.com/gdc-2024-game-audio-bundle/
  - Kenney audio packs (clean UI/game SFX, CC0):
    https://kenney.nl/assets/ui-audio
    Big all-in-one bundle (includes audio): https://kenney.itch.io/kenney-game-assets
  - itch.io asset packs (many ZIP bundles; free/paid, check each license):
    https://itch.io/game-assets/free/tag-sound-effects
    Example free bundle: https://tommusic.itch.io/free-fantasy-sfx-and-music-bundle
